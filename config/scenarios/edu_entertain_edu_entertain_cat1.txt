🐘 СЦЕНАРИЙ 1.1 — ANIMALS AND THEIR SOUNDS
Duration: 22 min | Age: 0–3 years
Concept
Each animal appears one at a time — name shown — sound played — environment shown. Parents see clear educational value. Children get visual + audio association. Slow pace — 45 seconds per animal — never rushed.
Song/Chant (repeating)
"The [animal] says [sound]! [Sound] [sound] [sound]! The [animal] says [sound]! All day long!"
Structure
Block
	Content
	Duration
	Intro
	Welcome song
	0:45
	Block A
	Farm animals (6)
	6:00
	Bridge
	All farm animals together
	1:30
	Block B
	Wild animals (6)
	6:00
	Bridge
	Animal parade
	1:30
	Block C
	Ocean + sky (5)
	5:00
	Finale
	All animals chorus
	1:15
	Animals + Sounds (45 sec each)
FARM ANIMALS
COW — MOO
* Large friendly cow walks in from left
* Stops center — faces camera — opens mouth
* MOO! — sound wave rings expand
* Text: COW / البقرة / Sapi (English/Arabic/Indonesian)
* Cow chews grass happily — tail swishes
* Small calf appears beside mama — both MOO together
SHEEP — BAA
* Fluffy white sheep bounces in
* Very fluffy — almost round — comic proportions
* BAA! — wool vibrates with sound
* Text appears: SHEEP / الخروف / Domba
* Sheep shakes head — wool flaps — BAA again
* Lamb peeks from behind parent
PIG — OINK
* Pink pig trots in cheerfully
* Curly tail spinning
* OINK OINK! — snout wiggles
* Text: PIG / الخنزير → GOAT for Arabic version
* Pig rolls in mud patch happily — OINK!
CHICKEN — CLUCK
* Yellow-orange chicken steps in — head bobbing
* CLUCK CLUCK CLUCK — fast and rhythmic
* Text: CHICKEN / الدجاجة / Ayam
* Lays an egg — looks surprised — proud!
* Chick hatches from egg — PEEP!
HORSE — NEIGH
* Majestic brown horse gallops across
* Slows and stops — faces camera proudly
* NEIGH! — mane flows dramatically
* Text: HORSE / الحصان / Kuda
* Stamps hoof — shakes mane — NEIGH again
DUCK — QUACK
* Familiar yellow duck waddles in (consistent character)
* QUACK QUACK QUACK!
* Text: DUCK / البطة / Bebek
* Jumps into small pond — splash — happy swim
________________


WILD ANIMALS
LION — ROAR
* Lion walks in regally — slow and proud
* Camera shakes slightly as ROAR builds
* ROARRR! — letters vibrate on screen
* Text: LION / الأسد / Singa
* Lion yawns — teeth visible — looks cute not scary
* Shakes mane — sits down like a big cat
ELEPHANT — TRUMPET
* Giant elephant stomps in — screen shakes
* Raises trunk high — PAAARP!
* Text: ELEPHANT / الفيل / Gajah
* Sprays water from trunk — rainbow in the mist
* Baby elephant peeks around leg
MONKEY — OOH OOH AHH
* Monkey swings in on vine — lands on branch
* OOH OOH AHH AHH! — scratches head
* Text: MONKEY / القرد / Monyet
* Picks banana — peels it — eats happily — OOH!
FROG — RIBBIT
* Green frog hops in — BOING BOING BOING
* RIBBIT! — throat puffs out
* Text: FROG / الضفدع / Katak
* Catches fly with tongue — HAP! — satisfied
SNAKE — HISSSS
* Snake slithers in gracefully — gentle S curves
* HISSSSS — tongue flicks
* Text: SNAKE / الثعبان / Ular
* Snake is friendly — winks — coils into circle
BEAR — GRRRR
* Bear lumbers in slowly — very fluffy
* GRRR! (soft, not scary) — then sneezes
* Text: BEAR / الدب / Beruang
* Bear finds honey — happy — licks paw
________________


OCEAN + SKY ANIMALS
FISH — BLUB BLUB
* Colorful fish swims in underwater scene
* BLUB BLUB BLUB — bubbles rise
* Text: FISH / السمكة / Ikan
* Fish blows bubble ring — another fish swims through it
DOLPHIN — CLICK CLICK
* Dolphin leaps from water — spins — back in
* Click click squeak! — sonar sounds
* Text: DOLPHIN / الدولفين / Lumba-lumba
* Does three flips — waves fin — dives
OWL — HOOT
* Owl sits on branch in moonlight
* Big eyes blink slowly — HOOOOOT!
* Text: OWL / البومة / Burung hantu
* Head rotates 180° — blinks — HOOT again
BIRD — TWEET
* Colorful tropical bird on branch — morning
* TWEET TWEET TWEET — very cheerful
* Text: BIRD / الطائر / Burung
* Flaps wings — flies up — circles — lands again
CAT — MEOW
* Fluffy cat stretches — yawns — sits prettily
* MEOW! — very clear — then purring PURRR
* Text: CAT / القطة / Kucing
* Washes face with paw — meows again
________________


Finale: All Animals Together (1:15)
* All animals appear simultaneously on screen
* Conductor penguin appears with baton
* On cue: all make their sounds together
* Beautiful musical chaos
* Each animal fades out with a wave
* Last one: duck — QUACK — winks — gone
Narrator lines
"The cow says MOO! Can you say MOO?" "The elephant says... listen carefully..." "All the animals are saying goodbye! Can you say goodbye?"

🌊 СЦЕНАРИЙ 1.2 — WHERE DO THEY LIVE?
Duration: 20 min | Age: 1–3 years
Concept
Five habitats — each introduced clearly — then animals that live there. Teaches categorization, environment, belonging. Very visual — habitat is the star, animals are guests.
Structure
Habitat
	Animals shown
	Duration
	Ocean / البحر / Laut
	Fish, dolphin, whale, crab, octopus
	4:00
	Forest / الغابة / Hutan
	Bear, deer, fox, owl, rabbit
	4:00
	Sky / السماء / Langit
	Eagle, butterfly, bee, bat, cloud
	4:00
	Desert / الصحراء / Gurun
	Camel, snake, scorpion (friendly), lizard
	4:00
	Home / البيت / Rumah
	Cat, dog, fish (pet), bird (pet), hamster
	4:00
	Per habitat sequence (4 min):
OCEAN (0:00–4:00)
Scene 1 (0:30): Habitat introduction
* Beautiful underwater scene fades in — blue, peaceful
* Coral, seaweed, light beams from surface
* Text: OCEAN / البحر / Laut — large and clear
* Slow pan across underwater world
Scene 2 (2:30): Animals arrive one by one Each animal (30 sec):
* Swims/moves into frame
* Name text appears: FISH / السمكة / Ikan
* Does one characteristic action
* Settles into background
* Next animal arrives
Scene 3 (1:00): All together
* All habitat animals visible in their environment
* Happy, content — this is HOME
* Narrator: "The ocean is their home! They love the ocean!"
FOREST (4:00–8:00)
* Deep green forest — dappled sunlight — birdsong
* Animals appear: bear from cave, deer from trees, fox from bushes
* Each named — each doing natural behavior
* "The forest is their home! They love the forest!"
SKY (8:00–12:00)
* Blue sky with clouds — birds and insects flying
* Eagle soars — butterfly floats — bee buzzes
* Bat shown at dusk — not scary — cute and fuzzy
* "The sky is their home! They love the sky!"
DESERT (12:00–16:00)
* Golden sand, cacti, hot sun, wavy heat
* Camel walks slowly — scorpion (cartoonish, friendly) scuttles
* Snake slithers in shade
* "The desert is their home! They love the desert!"
HOME (16:00–20:00)
* Cozy house interior — warm and familiar
* Cat sleeping, dog wagging tail, fish in bowl
* Pet bird in cage singing, hamster on wheel
* "Our home is THEIR home too! We take care of them!"
* Child character appears — pets the cat — cat purrs

🍂 СЦЕНАРИЙ 1.3 — FOUR SEASONS
Duration: 22 min | Age: 1–3 years
Concept
Same tree, same meadow — shown through all four seasons. Nature changes — colors change — animals change behavior. Teaches change, cycles, time. Beautiful visual storytelling — no words needed, but narration adds value.
Structure
Season
	Duration
	Colors
	Spring / الربيع / Musim Semi
	5:00
	Light green, pink, yellow
	Summer / الصيف / Musim Panas
	5:00
	Deep green, blue, gold
	Autumn / الخريف / Musim Gugur
	5:00
	Orange, red, brown
	Winter / الشتاء / Musim Dingin
	5:00
	White, blue, silver
	Full cycle repeat (fast)
	2:00
	All
	SPRING (0:00–5:00)
Scene 1 (1:00): The world wakes up
* Grey winter scene — bare tree, grey sky
* Slowly: first green bud appears on branch
* More buds — tiny pink blossoms
* Ground: first green grass shoots pushing through snow
* A small flower pushes through — opens — beautiful
* Text: SPRING / الربيع / Musim Semi
Scene 2 (2:00): Spring in full
* Tree covered in pink blossoms
* Butterflies arrive first
* Birds return — build nests — sing
* Baby animals born: lamb, foal, chick, rabbit kit
* Rain shower — rainbow after
* Everything fresh and new
Scene 3 (2:00): Spring activities
* Children character planting seeds
* Seeds germinate — visible roots underground
* Puddles forming — frog in puddle — RIBBIT
* Narrator: "Spring! Everything is growing and new!"
________________


SUMMER (5:00–10:00)
Scene 1 (1:00): Full bloom
* Same tree now full deep green leaves
* Bright blue sky — sunshine strong
* Wildflowers everywhere — bees busy
* Text: SUMMER / الصيف / Musim Panas
Scene 2 (2:00): Summer life
* Animals active — deer grazing — birds feeding chicks
* Butterflies at peak — colors most vivid
* River flowing — fish jumping
* Hot sun — animals in shade at midday
Scene 3 (2:00): Summer fun
* Children playing outside
* Ice cream melting in heat — funny
* Sunflowers turning to face sun
* Evening — fireflies appear
* Narrator: "Summer! Everything is warm and bright!"
________________


AUTUMN (10:00–15:00)
Scene 1 (1:00): Colors change
* Green leaves begin changing — one by one
* Yellow first — then orange — then red
* Most beautiful time — painterly quality
* Text: AUTUMN / الخريف / Musim Gugur
Scene 2 (2:00): Falling leaves
* Wind blows — leaves fall like rain
* Child catches leaf — looks at it
* Squirrel collecting nuts — preparing
* Birds forming V-shape flocks — migrating south
* Geese honking overhead
Scene 3 (2:00): Autumn harvest
* Vegetables ready: pumpkins, apples, corn
* Apples falling from tree — picking them up
* Animals getting fat — preparing for winter
* Days getting shorter — sun lower
* Narrator: "Autumn! The leaves are falling and changing color!"
________________


WINTER (15:00–20:00)
Scene 1 (1:00): First snow
* Same tree — bare branches
* First snowflake falls — then more
* Ground white — silent world
* Text: WINTER / الشتاء / Musim Dingin
Scene 2 (2:00): Winter world
* Icicles forming on branches — beautiful
* Fox tracks in snow — animal still active
* Birds at feeder — important scene for parents
* Bear sleeping in den — ZZZ
* Rabbit white now — camouflage
Scene 3 (2:00): Winter wonder
* Children making snowman
* Snowball fight — laughter
* Ice frozen on pond — careful!
* Hot cocoa inside — warmth after cold
* Stars very bright in winter sky
* Narrator: "Winter! Everything is quiet and white and beautiful!"
________________


FULL CYCLE FAST (20:00–22:00)
* Same single tree shown through all seasons
* 30 seconds per season — rapid beautiful change
* Time lapse feel — magical
* Final shot: full year complete — tree back to spring
* Narrator: "Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter — round and round!"

🌙 СЦЕНАРИЙ 1.4 — DAY AND NIGHT
Duration: 20 min | Age: 0–3 years
Concept
Same scene — same characters — shown during day and night. Introduces opposite concepts, time, routines. Very calming — night section helps with bedtime. Teaches: sun/moon, day animals/night animals, day activities/sleep.
Structure
Block
	Content
	Duration
	Sunrise intro
	Day begins
	1:00
	Morning
	Activities and animals
	4:00
	Midday
	Bright and busy
	3:00
	Sunset
	Transition
	2:00
	Night
	Stars, moon, night animals
	5:00
	Full cycle
	Fast day-night cycle
	3:00
	Lullaby outro
	Going to sleep
	2:00
	SUNRISE (0:00–1:00)
* Horizon glows orange-pink
* Sun peeks over — slowly rises
* One bird starts singing — then more join
* Dew on grass sparkles
* Rooster crows: COCK-A-DOODLE-DOO!
* Text: GOOD MORNING! / صباح الخير / Selamat pagi!
* Flower opens to greet the sun
MORNING (1:00–5:00)
Daytime characters wake up:
* Bird stretches wings on branch — first song
* Bee leaves hive — first flower visit
* Rabbit hops out of burrow — sniffs air
* Deer at stream — morning drink
* Butterfly emerges — wings dry in sun
* Children wake up — brush teeth — eat breakfast
Day activities:
* Flowers fully open — facing sun
* Bees busy — carrying pollen
* Birds feeding chicks — back and forth
* Squirrel active — busy collecting
* Dog playing in yard
* Everything bright and active
MIDDAY (5:00–8:00)
* Sun at highest point — brightest light
* Hot — animals in shade
* Cat sleeping in patch of sun
* Cicadas singing loudly
* Children playing outside
* Everything at maximum activity and brightness
SUNSET (8:00–10:00)
* Sun slowly lowering — turning orange-gold
* Shadows getting long
* Daytime animals heading home:
   * Birds returning to nests
   * Bees returning to hive
   * Rabbit back to burrow
   * Deer disappears into forest
* Sky colors: orange → pink → purple → dark blue
* Text: GOODNIGHT SUN / تصبح على خير أيها الشمس
NIGHT (10:00–15:00)
Night awakens:
* First star appears — then more — then many
* Moon rises — full and bright
* Owl opens eyes: HOOT HOOT
* Fireflies begin glowing
* Bat flutters out — very gentle, not scary
* Night flowers open: moonflower, night jasmine
* Cricket begins: CRICKET CRICKET
Night world:
* Moon moves slowly across sky
* Stars rotate slowly — Big Dipper visible
* Night animals busy:
   * Owl hunting silently
   * Hedgehog snuffling through leaves
   * Moon moths at flowers
   * Cat prowling (quietly)
* Everything silver-blue — magical
Night vs Day comparison:
* Split screen: daytime bee at flower / nighttime moth at flower
* Daytime bird singing / nighttime owl hooting
* Daytime rabbit / nighttime hedgehog
* "Different friends come out at night!"
FULL CYCLE FAST (15:00–18:00)
* Same scene cycling: dawn → morning → noon → afternoon → dusk → night → dawn
* Each phase 30 seconds — beautiful time lapse
* Music transitions with each phase
* 3 complete cycles
LULLABY OUTRO (18:00–20:00)
* Night scene — moon high — stars bright
* All daytime animals sleeping:
   * Bird head under wing in nest
   * Rabbit in burrow — ZZZ
   * Bear in den
   * Dog in kennel
* Child character in bed — eyes drooping
* Parents can use this to help bedtime
* Stars slowly fade
* Moon dims gently
* Complete darkness — one star twinkles
* Narrator (whisper): "Goodnight... goodnight... sleep tight..."

REMOTION NOTES — ALL 4 VIDEOS
Shared components needed:
* AnimalCharacter — base character with species prop
* HabitatBackground — environment with time-of-day prop
* TrilingualLabel — English/Arabic/Indonesian text display
* SoundWave — expanding rings from mouth on animal sound
* SeasonTransition — same scene morphing between seasons
* DayNightCycle — sky color and lighting transition system
