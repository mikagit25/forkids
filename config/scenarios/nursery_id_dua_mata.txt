👀 Dua Mata Saya — Full Video Script
Duration: ~22 minutes | Language: Bahasa Indonesia (vocals) + English subtitles Age: 0–3 years | Format: Remotion Purpose: Entertainment + body parts learning — classic Indonesian body parts nursery rhyme
________________


CORE SONG LYRICS
Bahasa Indonesia (vocals):
Dua mata saya Hidung saya satu Dua kaki saya Pakai sepatu baru Dua telinga saya Yang kiri dan kanan Satu mulut saya Tidak berhenti makan
English subtitles:
Two eyes of mine One nose of mine Two legs of mine Wearing new shoes Two ears of mine Left and right One mouth of mine Never stops eating

🎬 INTRO — 0:00–1:00
Animation
* Soft morning bedroom — warm sunrise light through curtains
* A cute round child character sleeping in bed
* Alarm clock rings — BRRRING!
* Character stirs — one eye opens slowly
* Then second eye opens — blinks twice
* Sits up — stretches arms — big yawn
* Looks in mirror next to bed — sees itself
* Waves at reflection — reflection waves back
* Jumps out of bed — full of energy
* Title appears body-part by body part, as character points: 👀 DUA — 👁️👁️ — MATA — SAYA DUA MATA SAYA!
Audio
* Soft morning sounds — birds outside
* BRRRING alarm
* Yawn sound
* Cheerful bouncy melody begins — 95 BPM, playful
Narrator (warm, clear voice):
"Selamat pagi teman-teman! Ayo kita pelajari bagian tubuh kita!" (Good morning friends! Let's learn about our body parts!)
English subtitle: Good morning friends! Let's learn about our body parts!

🎵 ROUND 1 — SLOW LEARNING VERSION — 1:00–5:30
Each body part introduced slowly and clearly.
________________


Line 1: Dua mata saya — 1:00–1:45
Animation:
* Character's face fills screen — friendly, round
* Two eyes highlighted with gentle glow circles
* Count: satu ← left eye glows | dua ← right eye glows
* Eyes blink one at a time — then together
* MATA = Eyes label appears
* DUA = 2 — number floats up with two eye icons
* Eyes go wide — then squint — then normal — showing range
Indonesian text: Dua mata saya English subtitle: Two eyes of mine
Singing: Clear, slow, pointing
Du-a... ma-ta... sa-ya...
Audio: Each "mata" — soft blink sound
________________


Line 2: Hidung saya satu — 1:45–2:25
Animation:
* Nose zooms into focus — wiggling slightly
* HIDUNG = Nose label
* SATU = 1 — one finger count
* Nose sniffs: SNIFF — flower appears — nose smells it — happy face
* Nose sniffs pepper: ACHOO! — tiny sneeze — stars fly out
* Character wipes nose — sheepish smile
Indonesian text: Hidung saya satu English subtitle: One nose of mine
Singing: Matter-of-fact, clear
Hi-dung... sa-ya... sa-tu...
Audio: SNIFF sound, then tiny ACHOO
________________


Line 3: Dua kaki saya — 2:25–3:05
Animation:
* Camera pans down to feet — two feet shown
* Count: satu ← left foot wiggles | dua ← right foot wiggles
* KAKI = Legs/Feet label
* Feet tap to the beat — left right left right
* Character does little dance steps
* Feet leave colorful footprints on floor
Indonesian text: Dua kaki saya English subtitle: Two legs of mine
Singing: Rhythmic, feet tapping
Du-a... ka-ki... sa-ya...
Audio: Tap tap tap of feet on floor
________________


Line 4: Pakai sepatu baru — 3:05–3:40
Animation:
* Spotlight on feet — old worn shoes there
* Magic sparkle — WHOOSH — NEW shiny shoes appear!
* SEPATU = Shoes label
* BARU = New — sparkle effect
* Character lifts feet one at a time showing off new shoes
* Shoes are bright red with stars on them
* Character does a little shoe-showing dance
Indonesian text: Pakai sepatu baru English subtitle: Wearing new shoes
Singing: Proud, happy
Pa-kai... se-pa-tu... ba-ru!
Audio: WHOOSH of new shoes appearing, sparkle sounds
________________


Line 5: Dua telinga saya — 3:40–4:15
Animation:
* Ears highlighted on each side of head
* Count: satu ← left ear | dua ← right ear
* TELINGA = Ears label
* Ears wiggle (like animals' ears) — very funny
* Sound waves appear going INTO ears
* Character hears music — ears perk up — nods to beat
* Cup hand to ear: listening pose
Indonesian text: Dua telinga saya English subtitle: Two ears of mine
Singing: Listening tone
Du-a... te-li-nga... sa-ya...
Audio: Soft sound effects entering ear visual
________________


Line 6: Yang kiri dan kanan — 4:15–4:40
Animation:
* Big LEFT arrow appears: KIRI ←
* Character turns left — left ear highlighted
* Big RIGHT arrow appears: KANAN →
* Character turns right — right ear highlighted
* Both arrows together — character's head swivels left-right
* Fun confusion — head spinning!
Indonesian text: Yang kiri dan kanan English subtitle: Left and right
Singing: Clear, directional
Yang... ki-ri... dan... ka-nan...
________________


Line 7: Satu mulut saya — 4:40–5:05
Animation:
* Mouth zooms in — big smile
* MULUT = Mouth label
* SATU = 1
* Mouth opens and closes — showing teeth — saying "AHHHH"
* Mouth sings a little — musical notes come out
* Mouth blows a kiss — heart floats up
Indonesian text: Satu mulut saya English subtitle: One mouth of mine
Singing: Clear, showing off
Sa-tu... mu-lut... sa-ya...
________________


Line 8: Tidak berhenti makan — 5:05–5:30
Animation:
* Character at table — FOOD appears — eyes light up
* Mouth CHOMPING — nom nom nom nom
* Food disappearing rapidly — comic eating speed
* More food appears — more eating
* MAKAN = Eating label
* Tummy grows slightly — satisfied rub
* Character: "Enak sekali!" (So delicious!)
Indonesian text: Tidak berhenti makan English subtitle: Never stops eating
Singing: Enthusiastic, funny
Ti-dak... ber-hen-ti... ma-kan!
Audio: NOM NOM NOM eating sounds — very funny

🎵 ROUND 3 — ACTIONS VERSION — 12:30–16:00
Action prompts — full body participation:
Dua mata saya:
* Point to own eyes with two fingers
* Prompt: "Tunjuk matamu! Satu... dua!" / Point to your eyes! One... two!
Hidung saya satu:
* Touch nose with one finger
* Prompt: "Sentuh hidungmu!" / Touch your nose!
Dua kaki saya:
* Stamp feet one at a time
* Prompt: "Injak! Satu! Dua!" / Stamp! One! Two!
Pakai sepatu baru:
* Look down at own shoes — show them off
* Prompt: "Tunjukkan sepatumu!" / Show your shoes!
Dua telinga saya:
* Pull ears gently — wiggle if possible
* Prompt: "Pegang telingamu! Kiri! Kanan!" / Hold your ears! Left! Right!
Yang kiri dan kanan:
* Turn head left then right
* Prompt: "Kiri! Kanan! Kiri! Kanan!" / Left! Right! Left! Right!
Satu mulut saya:
* Open mouth wide — say AHHHH
* Prompt: "Buka mulut! Ahhhh!" / Open your mouth! Ahhh!
Tidak berhenti makan:
* Pretend to eat — nom nom nom
* Prompt: "Makan! Nom nom nom!" / Eat! Nom nom nom!
* EVERYONE loves this one
________________


Song runs three times with actions — 12:30–16:00
First time: slow with prompts Second time: normal speed Third time: FAST — can kids keep up?!
Character gets progressively more exhausted doing all the actions Third time: panting but happy
