Happy Bear Kids ("we", "us", "our") operates educational YouTube channels and this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable laws, including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Content Directed at Children

Our YouTube channels publish educational videos intended for children under the age of 13, primarily babies and toddlers (ages 0–3). All videos are marked as "Made for Kids" on YouTube in compliance with COPPA requirements.

Because our content is directed at children, YouTube automatically applies restricted data settings to our videos. This means:

  • Personalised advertising is disabled on our videos
  • Comments are disabled on our videos
  • YouTube does not allow us to collect personal information from viewers of our videos

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any personal information from viewers of our YouTube videos or visitors to this website. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses or contact information
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Location data
  • Usage or behavioural data
  • Any information from children

3. This Website

This website is hosted by GitHub Pages and does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics tools or any third-party data collection services. No personal data is collected when you visit this site.

GitHub Pages may collect basic server logs (IP address, browser type, page visited) as part of their standard infrastructure. We do not have access to or control over this data. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

4. YouTube and Google

Our videos are hosted on YouTube (a Google service). YouTube's own privacy policy and terms of service apply when you watch our videos. Please review Google's Privacy Policy to understand how YouTube processes viewer data.

As noted above, because our content is marked "Made for Kids", YouTube applies additional protections and disables personalised advertising on all our videos.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to YouTube channels. Once you leave this website, the privacy practices of those third-party services apply. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

6. COPPA Compliance (USA)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our YouTube videos are designated "Made for Kids" which activates YouTube's COPPA-compliant data restrictions automatically.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at the email address below and we will take prompt action.

7. GDPR Compliance (European Union)

For users in the European Economic Area, we note that since we collect no personal data through this website or our YouTube content, there is no personal data processing for which we act as a data controller. YouTube's GDPR obligations apply to their platform separately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
